## WWII
- The "Big Three"

- Europe
    - Dwight D. Eisenhower, Supreme Commander of the Allied Force in Europe in D-day

- Pacific
    - Harry S. Truman
    - [General Douglas Macarthur](../people/macarthur_douglas.md)
